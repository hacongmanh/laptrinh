NOUNS

A noun represents a person,place,animal,thing or an idea

KINDS OF NOUN

Nouns are classified as follows

  • Pronper: It is the name of a particular object, whether person,place,animal,idea or thing
  • Common:It is the name possessed by thing
  • Abstract :It is the name representing qualities,emotions,conditions or actions

PRONOUNS

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun

KINDS OF PROMOUN

Pronuouns are classified as folloows

  • Personal pronouns :It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun
  • Indefinite pronouns:It stands as the answer to the question about a person,place or a thing
  • Indefinite pronouns :It is used to refer to a person,thing or a place that are unknown
  • Relative pronouns:It relates to noun, pronoun , word or expression preceding it in the sentence ,and at the samea time conects two statements
  • Demonstrative pronouns: A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it
  • Reflexive pronounsIt ususally follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
